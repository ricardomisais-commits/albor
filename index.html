Why I built this.

I have ADHD. And the thing that destroys my days most is waiting mode — that frozen state where I can't start anything because something is coming up later.

I could have 6 free hours before a meeting, but my brain treats them as unusable. I'd sit there, scrolling, watching the clock, feeling guilty for wasting time I couldn't make myself use.

Every productivity app I tried made it worse. More notifications. More streaks. More ways to feel like I wasn't doing enough.

So I built Albor. An app that shows me my actual free time, suggests gentle things if I want them, and genuinely lets me rest without making me feel bad about it.

I'm not a coder. I'm from Mexico. I used AI tools to help me build what I couldn't build alone. It took months. But it's here now, and it's free, because the people who need it most shouldn't have to pay to feel okay about their time.

— Ricardo
